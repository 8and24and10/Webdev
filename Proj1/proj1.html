Interactive Financial Dashboard

In an effort to combat income inequality, our team created a sentiment analysis model using FinBERT, a lexicon-based machine learning model, to democratize financial knowledge for the average investor to maximize their probability of financial success. By applying sentiment analysis, we will be able to calculate the current market sentiment based on financial news that the average investor ingests. To do so, we will implement the FinBERT model, a pre-trained NLP model using a vast financial corpus, thereby fine-tuning it for financial sentiment classification.

COVID-19 Data Modelling

This project is aimed to model the spread of COVID-19 in California State. In particular, we apply linear regression models to predict COVID-19 deaths for counties in California. Modeling and analysis have been carried out based on datasets detailing confirmed cases of COVID-19. Creating data-driven models enables us to prove if lockdown policies should be extended. It also has potential to provide estimates on how human activities will be directly affected by this disease in the future.

Social Network Rumors Detection

Since its inception, Social Media has been serving as a medium for spreading conspiracy theories, misinformation, and confusion and in recent times, Anti-vaccination movements have been gaining momentum since the approval of COVID vaccines. The individuals/communities who refuse to wear masks, typically do not believe in the effectiveness of masks and perceive it as a threat to their rights and some even believe that the COVID-19 pandemic is a hoax. The influencers in such communities often aggravate the situation by strengthening negative hashtag trends and can potentially become a hazard to public health. Thus, as a responsible social media platform (i.e. Twitter) we analyze and try to understand the network of anti-vaccination and anti-mask hashtags to determine an effective strategy to tackle these movements.
